Arngren.net Review

User Experience (UX)
  • The landing page of the website is confusing and messy.
    • The design is inconsistent and not pleasing to the eye since there are too much text and the alignment is close to nowhere.
  • The website is not responsive, it does not prioritize the needs of its users.
Content
  • The website as well as its content is unorganized thus making it unpleasant to the user to see.
  • Images as well as the texts are not used in a uniform manner in which it contains different sizes for the images and the texts use different colors, font style and sizes.
  • The components of the website are not arranged in a manner that is predetermined or aethetically structured.
Grid Layout
  • The grid is missing both margins and paddings, which results in the contents of the website being misaligned and unpleasant to look at.
  • There is no utilization of the grid structure and layout, therefore the information and components it contains are disorganized.
